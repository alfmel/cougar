INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS
-------------------------

Until we get the full PEAR package.xml defintion sorted out, install the Cougar
Framework as follows:

1. Copy cougar.php and Cougar subdirectory into your PEAR directory.

2. Copy or symlink the following files to /usr/bin or /usr/local/bin:

  Cougar\Model\cougar-model-builder
  Cougar\PDO\cougar-create-pdo-connection-file
  Cougar\Util\cougar-obfuscate-string

Enjoy!
